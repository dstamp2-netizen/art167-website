Design Process

My process helps me organize ideas and improve my pages each week.

  1. I brainstorm ideas and collect examples that match my goals.
  2. I plan the layout and write rough drafts of my content.
  3. I build the page with semantic HTML so it stays organized.
  4. I review and revise for readability & clarity.
